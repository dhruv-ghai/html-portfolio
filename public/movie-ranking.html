The Best Movies According to Dhruv

My top 3 movies of all-time.

"Can i eat your pancreas"

This is my favourite anime."Can I Eat Your Pancreas captivates with its poignant storytelling and heartfelt exploration of human connection."

"Avengers : Infinity war and endgame"

"Avengers: Infinity War delivers a breathtaking spectacle of superhero teamwork and epic battles." "Avengers: Endgame masterfully concludes the saga with emotional depth and grandeur."

"Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge"is a bollywood movie which beautifully captures the essence of love, tradition, and family values, all wrapped in a vibrant and heartfelt narrative.
